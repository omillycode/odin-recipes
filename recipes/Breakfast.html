Breakfast Sandwich

Description

A more modern sandwich, the breakfast sandwich gives you a whole new way to enjoy a meal.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • English Muffin
  • Bacon
  • Mayo
  • (Optionally) Sausage

Steps

  1. Fry a few slices of bacon until crispy
  2. Fry an egg in the bacon grease for extra flavor flav
  3. Toast the english muffin
  4. Prepare english muffin with first butter, and then mayo
  5. Finally, garnish your muffin with first the egg, and the bacon placed ontop.
  6. Enjoy the newest of the Big 3 Sandwich Legends
