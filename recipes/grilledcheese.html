Grilled Cheese

Description

A timeless classic enjoyed by all except the lactose intolerant.

A simple cheese slice melted between two pieces of bread grilled to perfection, and usually sliced diagonally.

This recipe works as a mid-night snack or for enjoying as lunch.

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice.
  • Cheese slices (Kraft, preferrably.)
  • Ketchup, salt and pepper.

Steps

  1. Fire up the grill.
  2. Melt butter on fired up grill.
  3. Layer as much cheese as you can fit between two slices of bread.
  4. Place the cheese-bread tower onto the fired up, melted butter grill.
  5. Wait impatiently or until first layers of cheese begin to melt.
  6. Flip over the sandwich and repeat step 5 until completely grilled.
  7. Place onto a plate with ketchup ON THE SIDE. (This is a critical step)
  8. Finally enjoy one of lifes finest treasures.
