Grilled Cheese

Description

A timeless classic enjoyed by all except the lactose intolerant.

A simple cheese slice melted between two pieces of bread grilled to perfection, and usually sliced diagonally.

This recipe works as a mid-night snack or for enjoying as lunch.

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice.
  • Cheese slices (Kraft, preferrably.)
  • Ketchup, salt and pepper.
