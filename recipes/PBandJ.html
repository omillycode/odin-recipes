Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

Another timeless classic, the peanut butter and jelly sandwich makes an excellent choice for either, breakfast, brunch, lunch, or (hopefully not) dinner.

Two pieces of bread slathered with creamy peanut butter, and sweet blueberry jam. This one is not to be missed

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Creamy Peanut Butter (crunchy can also be used, but why?)
  • Any sort of jelly or jam (blueberry recommended)
  • Possibly a third piece of bread, if you're feeling like and PBandJ Mac

Steps

  1. Take two pieces of bread (or three!)
  2. Spread peanut butter on one side, and jelly on the other side.
  3. Once an even amount has been distributed to both sides, introduce to the two sides to one another through a ceremonial smushing.
  4. Flatten the sandwich by hand to truly enjoy the recipe the way it was intended.
